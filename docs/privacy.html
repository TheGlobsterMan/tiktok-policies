Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 8, 2026

Burgerton ("we," "us," or "our") operates a content automation and scheduling application that integrates with TikTok and other social media platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Burgerton, we may collect:

  • Account information: Your TikTok username and user ID, obtained through the TikTok Login Kit.
  • Content data: Videos, captions, and scheduling metadata you provide for publication.
  • Usage data: Logs of actions taken within the application (e.g., posts scheduled, posts published).

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your account and connect to TikTok on your behalf.
  • To schedule and publish content you have approved.
  • To improve application reliability and performance.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required to operate the service (e.g., TikTok API) or as required by law.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. TikTok API Usage

Burgerton uses the TikTok API to publish content on your behalf. Your use of TikTok through this application is also subject to TikTok's Privacy Policy.

6. Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. OAuth tokens are stored securely and are never logged or shared.

7. Your Rights

You may revoke Burgerton's access to your TikTok account at any time through your TikTok app settings under "Manage app permissions."

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@burgerton.app
